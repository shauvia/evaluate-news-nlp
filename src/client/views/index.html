How to understand the result:

Score - polarity of the analysed text
  • Possible values:
  • P+ - strong positive
  • P - positive
  • NEU - neutral
  • N - negative
  • N+ - strong negative
  • NONE: without polarity
Agreement - marks the agreement between the sentiments detected in the text.
  • Possible values:
  • AGREEMENT - the different elements have the same polarity.
  • DISAGREEMENT - there is disagreement between the different elements' polarity.
